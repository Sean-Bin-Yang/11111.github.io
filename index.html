Biography

Sean Bin Yang is an Assistant Professor with the School of Advanced Manufacturing Engineering, Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunitions, Chognqing, China. Before joining CQUPT in 2022, he received his Ph.D degree in computer science from Aalborg University (AAU), Denmark. His supervisors is Prof. Bin Yang and Assoc. Prof. Jilin Hu. His was also a faculty member in the Center for Data-Intensive Systems (Daisy) and work with Prof. Christian S. Jensen. From Jun. 2021 to Dec. 2021, he was also supervised by Prof. Jian Tang. He is a Member of IEEE and ACM.

His research focuses on artificial intelligence and machine learning.

He has made contributions to advance path representation learning methods for real-life transportation applications, including travle cost estimation, path ranking, traffic analysis, and routing. His research has been published in top conferences and journals including KDD, ICDE, IJCAI, and TKDE.
